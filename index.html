An open source speedrunning game

Watch the trailer or download

Momentum is a game all about speed and shaving off seconds. Use classic fps movement such as bunnyhopping, surfing, and explosive jumping to maintain your momentum. Made by Eliot, Callum, Chris, Simon, Matt, and Regan.
